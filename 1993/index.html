The Finest Base #110 raw pulled back from $90 to $66, a correction that makes more sense given the PSA 9 is $221 — raw copies shouldn't be trading at a 75% discount to the graded version. Black Gold #33 PSA 9 jumped from $35 to $60 on a clean fixed-price sale. Plenty of new data points landed today that didn't exist yesterday — Leaf, Select Stars, Pinnacle inserts all getting their first PSA grades in the data.
