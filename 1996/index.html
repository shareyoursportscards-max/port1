Recent 1996 Market Reports

July 4, 2026The rarest cards in 1996 don't trade often — but when they do, they rewrite the record book. The Select Certified Mirror Blue /45 finally showed its hand: a raw copy went to $4,496 at auction with 41 bids, the first ungraded data point ever on a card where only 45 exist. Twenty minutes of bidding told us more about that card than two years of guessing. Same story with the Topps Chrome Refractor #70 — the original '96 Chrome refractor had never shown a PSA 9 price until one hammered at $3,905 with 36 bids. And the Ultra Hitting Machines die-cut, already one of the toughest pulls of the year at 1:288, landed its first PSA 9 at $1,259. Underneath the fireworks, the serial-numbered Donruss stuff keeps waking up: Power Alley /5,000 raw jumped from $95 to $234 and the Summit Big Bang /600 more than tripled from $70 to $232. Not everything cooperated — Studio Stained Glass Stars raw slid from $150 to $114 on back-to-back sales. But the theme of the day was discovery: three cards that never had a market price before now have serious ones.
June 29, 2026Studio Press Proof Silver #116 wasn't even in this guide 24 hours ago. Then it sold for $2,828 with 16 bids. That's what happens when a low-pop parallel from a forgotten set suddenly gets discovered — a card that doesn't even show up in most price searches just repriced the whole Studio category overnight. Meanwhile the refractor market stayed hot: Finest Refractor #305 PSA 8 pushed through $400 for the first time, and the Wrecking Crew Refractor PSA 8 jumped $87 to $350 — two Topps Chrome refractors from '96 that have been quietly gaining for months. The Metal Universe Platinum Edition raw doubled from $150 to $330, which tells you something about what serious collectors think that card is worth ungraded. Not everything went up — Power Alley PSA 9 dropped hard from $500 to $199, erasing months of gains in one sale. The 1996 market is full of these extremes right now: underappreciated cards exploding, overpriced cards resetting. The Studio Press Proof Silver is exactly why you watch the sold listings every day.
