Griffey Card Market Report — June 22, 2026

What moved in the Ken Griffey Jr. card market on June 22, 2026, based on real eBay sold listings across 10 years.

1990

Leaf #245 PSA 9 jumped from $20 to $30 — a 50% move on the card most collectors consider the best base Griffey of 1990. Major League Debut PSA 9 dropped from $60 to $42. Added Bowman Tiffany PSA 8 at $70 — first PSA 8 data point on that card.

1991

Desert Shield All-Star #392 PSA 8 ticked up from $575 to $590. Desert Shield cards continue to hold strong — military-only distribution means supply isn't getting any bigger.

1992

Ultra #123 PSA 10 moved from $70 to $77 at auction with 15 bids, while PSA 9 softened from $27 to $23. The gap between 9 and 10 is widening on early Ultra — gem copies are getting scarcer.

1993

Select #2 PSA 10 climbed from $61 to $75. Ultra #619 PSA 10 dipped from $75 to $69 at auction. Mixed signals in '93 — some gems gaining, others giving back.

1994

Added Flair Hot Gloves #3 PSA 8 at $300 with a die-cut tag — first PSA 8 data on one of the most beautiful die-cut inserts of the decade. The PSA 9 sits at $325 so the 8-to-9 gap is tight on this one.

1995

Flair Outfield Power #7 PSA 9 moved from $50 to $61 at auction with 13 bids. Flair inserts from '95 are quietly gaining — the Hot Glove, Hot Numbers, and Outfield Power set is underrated.

1996

Finest Sterling Refractor #24 climbing — PSA 9 moved from $490 to $536 at auction with 38 bids, and PSA 8 jumped from $250 to $370. On the other side, Metal Universe Mother Lode PSA 9 pulled back from $191 to $165, and Ultra Call to the Hall Gold Medallion PSA 9 dropped from $337 to $285. Mystery Finest Refractor #M25 PSA 8 also cooled from $152 to $100.

1997

E-X 2000 Hall or Nothing PSA 9 surged from $375 to $605 at auction with 29 bids — a 61% jump. The die-cut design on this card is iconic and demand is clearly accelerating. SPx Cornerstones of the Game (w/ Bonds) PSA 8 softened from $350 to $300.

1998

Metal Universe Hardball Galaxy filled in PSA 9 at $200 — a sci-fi design that's becoming increasingly collectible. The rest of '98 held steady today.

1999

Topps Chrome All-Etch Refractor #AE3 PSA 8 jumped from $260 to $350 — demand is climbing across all grades on this card. Added Finest Prominent Figures Refractor (/457) PSA 10 at $750 and Topps Tek Gold (/10) PSA 9 at $676 — a POP 1 sale with 20 bids.
