Griffey Card Market Report — June 25, 2026

What moved in the Ken Griffey Jr. card market on June 25, 2026, based on real eBay sold listings across 5 years.

1991

Desert Shield #790 PSA 9 pushed higher to $1,900 — up from $1,750. The Desert Shield premium keeps expanding. Quiet otherwise in '91.

1996

Flair Hot Gloves raw climbed from $200 to $265. Bowman's Best Cuts Atomic Refractor raw surged from $400 to $600 — a 50% jump on one of the most sought-after die-cuts in '96. Stadium Club Extreme Winners Gold landed at raw $676 — first data on that card. Metal Universe cooled: Mother Lode raw dipped to $85 and Platinum Edition raw eased to $120.

1997

Flair Showcase Row 0 PSA 9 dropped from $810 to $679. Blast Furnace PSA 10 gave back gains, falling from $585 to $530. Magnetic Field raw collapsed from $153 to $79. On the flip side, Flair Showcase Diamond Cuts raw climbed from $375 to $475. Correction day for Metal Universe, but Flair Showcase inserts are holding strong.

1998

Finest Centurion (/500) raw landed at $898 at auction with 27 bids — first raw data on this card. Diamond Heroes PSA 9 ticked up from $450 to $465. Metal Universe Titanium raw eased from $384 to $320.

1999

Skybox Thunder Turbocharged raw exploded from $50 to $1,524 at auction with 39 bids — a 30x move that rewrites the book on this card. Molten Metal Fusion Sterling (/500) raw landed at $2,250. Soul of the Game raw nearly tripled from $65 to $170. The rare Skybox inserts from '99 are being discovered and repriced in real time.
