Griffey Card Market Report — June 27, 2026

What moved in the Ken Griffey Jr. card market on June 27, 2026, based on real eBay sold listings across 6 years.

1994

Finest Base #232 raw nearly doubled from $17 to $30 — demand is spreading beyond the refractor. Dugout Dirt #7 PSA 10 climbed from $95 to $125. Added Dugout Dirt Members Only with PSA 8 at $31 and PSA 9 at $65. Filled in blanks on Pinnacle Museum Collection raw $32, Select Crown Contenders raw $16, Leaf Slideshow raw $8, and Topps Black Gold PSA 8 at $18.

1995

The Bubble Gum card is the most liquid card in the hobby right now — 15+ raw sales in two days, all landing in the $40-$50 range. Raw ticked up from $41 to $46. Select Big Sticks raw up from $52 to $58. Leaf Limited Gold #6 PSA 9 jumped from $85 to $105. Filled in Flair Hot Glove raw at $42 and Studio Platinum raw at $50.

1996

Bowman's Best Cuts Atomic Refractor PSA 8 more than doubled from $400 to $925 at auction with 17 bids. Hitting Machines raw surged from $200 to $429 at auction with 37 bids — that die-cut is getting chased hard. Best Cuts Refractor raw climbed from $125 to $165. Platinum Edition raw up to $150. Summit Above and Beyond filled in raw at $93 and PSA 9 jumped from $100 to $130. On the other side, Titanium raw corrected from $44 to $30 with multiple lower sales, and Ultra Call to the Hall raw dropped from $13 to $6. The Bowman's Best die-cuts are the hottest cards in 1996 right now.

1997

Correction day for 1997 Ultra raws. Thunder Clap raw dropped from $548 to $365 at auction with 47 bids. Home Run Kings raw fell from $385 to $206 at auction with 34 bids. Sweet Strokes raw continued sliding to $125. Peter Max raw dropped from $100 to $59 at auction. But blanks are filling fast — Knot-Hole Gang raw landed at $266, Baseball Rules raw at $136, Best Cuts Refractor BC6 raw at $175, and Magnetic Field bounced from $40 back to $47. The raw market is repricing hard while graded holds steady.

1998

Ultra Diamond Immortals PSA 9 sold for $2,700 at auction with 38 bids — first PSA 9 data and a monster number. Raw also surged from $514 to $900. Platinum Portraits raw up from $213 to $260. Hardball Galaxy raw climbed from $19 to $30. Filled in Diamond Heroes raw at $70, Mystery Finest Bordered Refractor raw at $120, Milestone Refractor PSA 8 at $106, and Masterstrokes raw at $41. Lots of blanks filled today — the collection tracker is getting more useful by the hour.

1999

Blank-fill day across 1999. Gallery of Heroes raw climbed from $134 to $203 at auction. EX Century raw up from $30 to $45. Filled in Bowman Chrome Lasting Impact Refractor raw at $300, All-Topps Mystery Finest Refractor raw at $175, Topps Chrome All-Etch raw at $61, Ionix Cyber raw at $45, Brilliants Shining Stars raw at $35, Stadium Club Chrome raw at $30, and Starquest Red raw at $25. On the downside, Topps Lords of the Diamond raw dropped from $46 to $21 and Finest Milestone Refractor PSA 9 fell from $130 to $73. Massive session for filling portfolio-ready cells.
