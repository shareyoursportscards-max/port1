Griffey Card Market Report — July 8, 2026

What moved in the Ken Griffey Jr. card market on July 8, 2026, based on real eBay sold listings across 6 years.

1994

Flair Hot Gloves #3 PSA 10 jumped from $750 to $1,200 — the third grade on this die-cut to move up in three weeks. Finest Base #232 PSA 10 climbed from $450 to $550, with five raw sales in four days confirming $31. Stadium Club Finest #5 PSA 9 rose from $52 to $84 at auction with 21 bids, and Select Crown Contenders raw moved from $16 to $78. Leaf Statistical Standouts PSA 10 dipped from $550 to $480.

1995

Eleven Pinnacle Bubble Gum sales in four days moved every grade: raw from $50 to $65, PSA 8 from $109 to $155 at auction, and PSA 9 to $265 across four sales. The Museum Collection parallel filled in raw at $375. Finest Base #118 got its first PSA 10 sale at $850 (POP 17), and Flair Base #81 PSA 10 rose to $310 while its PSA 9 doubled to $65. Flair Hot Numbers PSA 9 fell from $200 to $149, and Topps #397 PSA 10 dropped from $196 to $140 on back-to-back sales.

1996

Metal Universe Platinum Edition raw dropped hard — four sales in four days between $74 and $150 brought it from $330 to $81, and PSA 8 followed from $400 to $150. Elsewhere prices climbed: Flair Diamond Cuts #5 PSA 10 sold for $2,127 at auction, Leaf Limited Lumberjacks #1 PSA 10 jumped from $325 to $500, and Finest Sterling Refractor #24 raw doubled from $200 to $400 on back-to-back sales. Topps Chrome #70 PSA 9 hit $100 for the first time.

1997

Bowman Chrome Refractor #12 PSA 9 jumped from $536 to $949. Flair Showcase Diamond Cuts #5 climbed in two grades — PSA 8 to $510 and PSA 9 from $525 to $750, both at auction. Bowman's Best International Atomic Refractor PSA 8 rose to $609 with 27 bids. On the downside, Flair Showcase Row 0 raw fell from $640 to $425 and Goudey Greats PSA 9 dropped from $495 to $340. Leaf Dress for Success /3,500 split: PSA 9 up to $200, PSA 10 down to $675.

1998

First PSA 9 data on the Stadium Club Triumvirate Luminescent landed at $1,780, and the Ultra Win Now acetate got its first PSA 9 at $300. Leaf Fractal Materials /250 raw ticked up to $207 at auction with 18 bids, and Metal Universe Universal Language PSA 10 reached a new high at $300. Corrections: Fleer Tradition Zone #7 raw eased from $240 to $203, Finest Centurion raw to $165, and Diamond Heroes raw to $75.

1999

Skybox Thunder Dial 1 #3D PSA 10 doubled from $1,350 to $2,700 — a 1:300 pull that rarely trades in gem. Molten Metal Xplosion #133 PSA 10 nearly tripled from $185 to $522 at auction with 31 bids, and Topps Chrome All-Etch Refractor raw climbed to $545. Skybox Thunder Turbocharged #3 raw dropped from $1,524 to $140. Flair Showcase Measure of Greatness /500 filled in raw at $400 with PSA 8 up to $490, Bowman Chrome Diamond Aces Refractor PSA 9 rose to $400, and UD View to a Thrill Double PSA 9 jumped from $77 to $219.
