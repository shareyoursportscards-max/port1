Griffey Card Market Report — July 14, 2026

What moved in the Ken Griffey Jr. card market on July 14, 2026, based on real eBay sold listings across 4 years. Price moves are sale to sale, not averages.

1996

A raw Pinnacle Skylines #1 sold for $1,200. The Bowman's Best Preview Refractor #BBP30 PSA 9 climbed from $225 to $450 (up 100%) at auction, while the Preview Atomic Refractor #BBP30 eased from $645 to $475 (down 26%). Stadium Club Extreme Winners Gold #EW8 raw climbed from $676 to $850 (up 26%). Metal Universe was mixed — Platinum Edition #107 raw rebounded from $70 to $140 (up 100%), Base #107 raw from $33 to $48, and Heavy Metal #4 from $47 to $52, but Base #107 PSA 8 slipped from $150 to $125 and Mother Lode #3 PSA 8 from $64 to $46. Select En Fuego #1 raw moved from $265 to $290 and Leaf Hats Off #5 raw from $50 to $60. On the downside, Topps Sweet Strokes raw gave back its recent gain from $160 to $130, Ultra Diamond Producers Gold Medallion #3 raw from $100 to $80, and Extreme Winners Silver #EW8 raw from $55 to $43.

1997

The Bowman's Best Atomic Refractor Preview #BBP2 PSA 10 jumped from $1,100 to $2,550 (up 132%) at auction with 42 bids. The E-X 2000 A Cut Above #2 PSA 8 climbed from $3,252 to $4,600 (up 41%), and the Metal Universe Mother Lode raw jumped from $925 to $1,400 (up 51%). Fleer Diamond Tributes #4 raw rose from $436 to $740 (up 70%) in a 43-bid auction, and Topps Gallery of Heroes #GH4 PSA 9 moved from $525 to $721 (up 37%). Raw prices firmed across the year: Metal Universe Titanium from $81 to $124, Ultra Baseball Rules! #2 from $80 to $120, Topps Sweet Strokes #SS6 from $125 to $160, Gallery Peter Max #PM5 from $59 to $90, E-X 2000 base #40 back from $65 to $85, and Bowman's Best International Atomic Refractor #BBI2 from $610 to $650. A raw Finest Gold Embossed #342 sold for $90. Pulling back: Ultra Thunder Clap #8 raw from $800 to $700 and Fleer Night and Day raw from $108 to $89.

1998

The Ultra Gold Medallion PSA 9 pushed from $950 to $1,225 (up 29%), though its raw eased from $179 to $160. A PSA 8 Finest Refractor #100 sold for $309 in a 24-bid auction. Topps Lords of the Diamond #LD1 raw ticked up from $35 to $40. Softening: Topps Chrome Flashback Refractor #FB2 raw from $230 to $140 (down 39%), and Metal Universe Hardball Galaxy gave back more of its recent run — raw from $19 to $16 and PSA 8 from $60 to $55 across a stack of sales.

1999

The Skybox Premium Soul of the Game #9 PSA 9 climbed from $950 to $1,100 (up 16%). A raw Bowman's Best Refractor /400 sold for $1,025 in a 14-bid auction. Ultra Thunder Clap #10 raw moved from $280 to $325, Ultra base #72 PSA 9 from $13 to $30, and Fleer Brilliants Shining Stars #1 raw from $35 to $38. On the other side, Skybox Thunder Turbocharged #3 raw fell from $140 to $100 (down 29%) at auction and Topps Chrome All-Etch #AE3 raw from $61 to $46.
