Griffey Card Market Report — July 19, 2026

What moved in the Ken Griffey Jr. card market on July 19, 2026, based on real eBay sold listings across 6 years. Price moves are sale to sale, not averages.

1993

A Topps Black Gold #33 sold for $599 in PSA 10, and the Stadium Club Members Only #59 PSA 10 jumped from $401 to $589 (up 47%). The Donruss Diamond Kings #DK-1 PSA 10 eased from $1,500 to $1,176 in a 24-bid auction.

1994

The Flair Hot Gloves #3 PSA 10 climbed from $1,200 to $1,300 at auction. An Ultra Award Winners #6 sold for $325 in PSA 10 with 47 bids, and the Studio #101 PSA 10 rose from $240 to $280.

1995

The Pinnacle #128 Bubble Gum stayed the most active card in the set. The PSA 10 edged from $2,745 to $2,800, the PSA 9 from $298 to $309 in a 19-bid auction, and raw copies climbed from $55 to $80 (up 45%) on contested auctions at $80 and $81. A Pinnacle White Hot #WH2 sold for $1,225 in PSA 9. Select Certified moved at the top: Gold Team #1 PSA 9 from $415 to $515 (up 24%) and Mirror Gold #70 PSA 9 from $504 to $525 in a 29-bid auction. A Bowman's Best Red Refractor #49 raw sold for $270 after 31 bids. Pulling back: Leaf Statistical Standouts /5,000 raw from $236 to $180 and Swing Men Museum Collection #304 raw from $110 to $100.

1997

The Score Showcase Series Artist's Proof #156 PSA 10 pushed from $1,500 to $1,775, and the Topps Hobby Masters #HM1 PSA 10 from $550 to $715 (up 30%). The Flair Showcase Diamond Cuts #5 raw eased from $475 to $395 in a 42-bid auction.

1998

A 34-bid auction pushed the Topps Chrome Refractor #321 PSA 10 from $4,150 to $6,223 (up 50%), a new high and the top '98 sale of the day. The base Topps #321 PSA 10 also climbed, from $138 to $216 (up 57%) with 18 bids. Raw prices firmed across Metal Universe: All-Galactic Team from $221 to $800 (up 262%), Titanium from $275 to $316 in a 39-bid auction, and Diamond Heroes from $45 to $86 with 23 bids, alongside a PSA 8 at $130. Ultra Gold Medallion raw rose from $160 to $225, Diamond Producers raw from $275 to $299, and Topps Gallery of Heroes #GH1 raw from $550 to $638. The Topps Mystery Finest Bordered Refractor #M20 PSA 8 climbed from $90 to $200 (up 122%). A raw Donruss Elite Master Craftsmen /100 sold for $1,009, a SPx Finite Radiance /1,000 raw for $124, and a Topps Inter-League Mystery Finest Refractor #ILM10 raw for $90. Cooling: Finest Centurion /500 raw from $170 to $143 at auction, and Flair Showcase Row 1 #1 raw from $271 to $175 (down 35%). Lower down, the base Metal Universe #161 had a PSA 8 sell at $66, E-X2001 #10 raw firmed from $35 to $42 in a 16-bid auction, and Topps Lords of the Diamond #LD1 raw eased from $40 to $35.

1999

Graded inserts led 1999. The Molten Metal Xplosion #133 PSA 9 jumped from $51 to $242 (up 375%) in a 31-bid auction, and the Topps Chrome All-Etch Refractor #AE3 PSA 9 climbed from $475 to $800 (up 68%). The Bowman Chrome Refractor #52 PSA 9 rose from $650 to $799 with 28 bids, and the Upper Deck Game Jersey #KG-H PSA 9 from $760 to $886 in a 24-bid auction. The Topps Chrome Lords of the Diamond Refractor #LD1 PSA 8 moved from $330 to $450, with raw copies from $338 to $350. Raw prices firmed on the scarce inserts: Metal Universe Diamond Soul from $431 to $590 (up 37%), Skybox Premium Soul of the Game from $300 to $366 in a 21-bid auction, and Topps Gallery Gallery of Heroes #GH3 from $175 to $220. A Bowman Chrome Diamond Aces Refractor #DA11 raw sold for $216 after a 34-bid auction, a Skybox Thunder Hip-No-Tized #4 raw for $144, and the Bowman's Best Franchise Best Mach III /500 sold at $400 raw and $1,000 in PSA 9. Cooling: Ultra Thunder Clap #10 raw from $325 to $260 and Stadium Club Video Replay #VR3 PSA 10 from $134 to $127.
